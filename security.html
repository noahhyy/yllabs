Security overview

YLLABS keeps your ER:LC server resilient against raids, nukes, floods, and spam—without slowing down trusted members.

Instant guard modes

One-command lockdowns for raids, nukes, and floods. Scopes to risky events and preserves access for trusted roles.

Permission audits

Automatic checks for dangerous role combos, public channels, insecure webhooks, and overbroad bot permissions.

Rate limiting

Adaptive throttles on joins, messages, reactions, and invite creation to neutralize mass spam without harming normal activity.

Webhook shield

Detects and disables compromised webhooks. Optional whitelist mode for known endpoints only.

Anti-nuke controls

Prevents mass channel deletions, role wipes, and permission escalations with automatic rollbacks and alerts.

Join flood defense

Patterns-based join analysis with cooldowns, CAPTCHA/verification prompts, and auto-mute for fresh accounts.

Anomaly detection

Flags unusual spikes in invites, DMs, mentions, or emoji usage. Sends structured alerts with recommended actions.

Evidence-first logging

Immutable logs with timestamps, IDs, and context for every action. Exportable for appeals and staff reviews.

Incident playbooks

Guided steps for mods during raids: quarantine channels, freeze invites, isolate risky roles, and restore safely.

Best practices with YLLABS

  • Role hygiene: Limit admin-equivalent permissions; split duties between moderation and operations.
  • Channel visibility: Avoid public @everyone write access; enforce slow mode in high-traffic areas.
  • Bot scopes: Grant least-privilege and rotate tokens if any suspicious activity is detected.
  • Verification: Use staged onboarding with CAPTCHA or manual review for high-risk waves.
  • Backups & exports: Regularly export configs and logs for quick restore and audit compliance.

FAQs

Will lockdown disrupt normal members?

Lockdown targets only the attack surface. Trusted roles retain expected access while risky vectors are isolated.

Do I need verification enabled all the time?

No. Enable it dynamically during join floods or high-risk events to reduce friction for legitimate users.

Can I export security logs?

Yes. Export structured evidence (timestamps, IDs, actions) for appeals, audits, or external review.
